# [Project Name] — LinkedIn Personal Brand Copilot  
*(with Persistent Cognition)*

## What this is

A **deeply personal**, memory-first copilot designed to help thoughtful professionals maintain **continuity of thought**, **identity coherence**, and **long-term intellectual positioning** on LinkedIn.

It treats your personal brand not as a content farm, but as a **living cognitive process** — one that should become more refined, more consistent, and more authentically *you* the longer you use it.

Core philosophy in one sentence:  
**Memory > Generation**

The system succeeds **only if** the quality, depth, and fidelity of its understanding of you improves meaningfully over time.

## Product Definition & Non-Negotiables

This project is built around three interlocking constraints that are **not up for debate**:

1. **Continuity of Thought**  
   Every interaction should feel like a continuation of the previous one — no amnesia, no tedious re-explaining of context, values, voice, or long-term positioning.

2. **Identity Preservation**  
   The system must fiercely protect the uniqueness of your voice, worldview, intellectual boundaries, and evolving narrative — even (especially) when suggesting phrasing or structure.

3. **Long-term Coherence**  
   Brand-building here is understood as **decades-long signal creation**, not weekly dopamine hits. The copilot optimizes for compounding clarity and reputation over years, not virality over days.

→ If the memory layer doesn't demonstrably improve after 3–6 months of real usage → the entire system is considered failing.

## What this is NOT

Let's be brutally clear so we waste nobody's time:

- Not a growth hacking bot  
- Not an autoposter / scheduler  
- Not a “viral template” / hook generator factory  
- Not autonomous in v1 (and probably never fully autonomous — you're the author, not the product)

If it even **smells** like spam, automation theater, or inauthentic signal farming → it's deliberately kept out.

## Non-goals (v1)

These are **explicitly deprioritized** — not "nice to have later", but active **anti-priorities** in the first major version(s):

- UI polish / fancy frontend  
- Full end-to-end automation  
- Vanity metrics dashboards & analytics porn  
- Multi-platform support (Twitter/X, Threads, Substack, etc.)

You are **not** building a startup demo.  
You are **not** building another content mill SaaS.  
You are building **persistent, private cognition** that augments a human being's long-term signaling ability.

Everything else is secondary or actively harmful distraction at this stage.
